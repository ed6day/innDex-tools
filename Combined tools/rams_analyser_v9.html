RAMS Analyser

Upload one or more site people workbooks and multiple RAMS report Excel files. The site people workbooks can be either employee training records or innDex time management downloads. The tool extracts RAMS titles, revisions, briefing dates and signed attendees, then compares them against the uploaded site attendance list to show who has attended site and which risk assessments they are signed up to.
